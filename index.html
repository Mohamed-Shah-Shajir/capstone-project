Nestled in the serene hills of the Western Ghats, Kuttikkanam is a picturesque hill station in Kerala, renowned for its tranquil ambiance and lush greenery. Located in the Idukki district, this charming destination is a haven for nature lovers, adventure enthusiasts, and anyone seeking a refreshing escape from the bustling city life.

Kuttikkanam is surrounded by mist-covered tea plantations, dense pine forests, and cascading waterfalls, making it a visual delight throughout the year. It is a perfect spot for trekking, camping, and exploring the rich biodiversity of the region. The cool climate and pristine air add to its charm, drawing visitors from far and wide.
